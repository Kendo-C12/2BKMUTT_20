King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi (KMUTT), also known as "Bangmod," is a university in Thailand that is well known for engineering and technology.
KMUTT’s main campus is located in Thung Khru, and it also has a Bang Khun Thian campus, which is home to the Faculty of Architecture and Design.
In addition, KMUTT offers the "2B-KMUTT" program, a research camp that gives high school students the opportunity to experience real research work at the university.
